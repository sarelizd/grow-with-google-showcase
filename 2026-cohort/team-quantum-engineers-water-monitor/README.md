# Hyperlocal Water Quality Monitor Portal

**Team Name:** Quantum Engineers  
**UN SDG Goal:** Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation  

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
Community members lack a central, accessible public dashboard to view municipal water testing results and safety warnings.

## 🎯 Project Overview
This portal provides localized, transparent, and up-to-date information on municipal water safety returns to empower residents and ensure community-wide access to reliable clean water data.
